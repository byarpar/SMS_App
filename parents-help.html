How Parents Can Help

Top Tips for Parents

  1. Open Communication

    Maintain an open dialogue with your teens about their online activities. Create a safe space for them to share their experiences and concerns.

  2. Set Clear Guidelines

    Establish rules for social media use, including time limits and appropriate content. Make sure these rules are understood and agreed upon by your teens.

  3. Education

    Learn about the apps and platforms your teens are using. Stay informed about new trends and potential risks in social media.

  4. Privacy Settings

    Help your teens set up and understand privacy settings on their accounts. Regularly review these settings together as they can change with app updates.

  5. Lead by Example

    Model responsible social media use in your own online activities. Show your teens how to engage positively and safely on social platforms.
