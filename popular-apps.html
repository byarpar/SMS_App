Most Popular Social Media Apps

Instagram

A photo and video-sharing social networking service owned by Facebook, Inc.

Safety Tips:

  • Set your account to private
  • Be careful with location sharing
  • Use the 'Close Friends' feature for more private sharing
  • Be aware of and report inappropriate content

TikTok

A video-sharing social networking service owned by ByteDance, a Chinese company.

Safety Tips:

  • Use privacy settings to control who can see your content
  • Be cautious about participating in challenges and trends
  • Limit screen time and take breaks
  • Don't share personal information in videos

Snapchat

A multimedia messaging app developed by Snap Inc.

Safety Tips:

  • Use 'Ghost Mode' to hide your location
  • Be cautious about who you add as friends
  • Remember that 'disappearing' messages can be saved by others
  • Use the 'Report' feature for any concerning content
